Project Summary

“My Medical Secretary’ (MMS) is a mobile application designed for iOS, android and tablet users. It is being designed for patients who have been diagnosed with a serious illness, such as cancer, and help them organise and understand all of the information and appointments surrounding their diagnosis, which, in return, reduce patient confusion and queries. “
